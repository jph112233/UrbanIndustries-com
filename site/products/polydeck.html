What Is Polydeck

The Footer Problem - Solved

Polydeck is a laminated flooring panel that eliminates the need for footers extending below frost lines. It rests on standard foundation supports and creates an ideal attachment surface for enclosure construction.

Simple, economical, and engineered for easy installation - Polydeck gets your project off the ground faster.

  • Rests on 4" x 4" and 2" x 6" foundation supports
  • Meets footer requirements without deep excavation
  • Ideal attachment surface for enclosures
  • Strong, warm, above-ground installation
  • Minimal construction time
