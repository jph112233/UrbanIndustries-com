What Are SIPs

The High-Performance Building System

SIPs feature an insulating foam core sandwiched between structural facings, typically oriented strand board (OSB). Factory manufacturing enables customization for virtually any building design while delivering exceptional strength, energy efficiency, and cost-effectiveness.

Urban Industries is a premier SIP panel manufacturer in Ohio - helping builders create warm, healthy environments with safe, cost-effective materials.

Why Choose SIPs

Cost, Performance & Green Building

Cost Competitive

SIPs construction costs approximately the same as traditional wood frame when accounting for labor savings and shorter timelines.

Energy Efficient

Reduce heat loss by establishing a continuous barrier of rigid foam insulation, cutting HVAC system requirements and operating costs.

Healthy Spaces

Healthy indoor environments free from mold and VOCs. Responsible material and resource conservation for green building certification.

Green Building

Environmental Benefits

SIPs support responsible construction and green building initiatives.
