Four Problems. One Solution.

What NewRoof Fixes

Roof Leaks

NewRoof's contoured aluminum panels seal over existing roofing with fasteners secured through the home's side - not penetrating the roof surface.

Roof Rumble

Polystyrene insulation layer dampens noise and vibration, eliminating the disruptive "rumble" sound common in aging mobile home roofs.

Condensation

The insulation barrier prevents temperature differentials that cause moisture condensation on ceilings and walls.

High Energy Bills

Two-inch polystyrene insulation substantially reduces heating and cooling expenses throughout the year.

How It Works

Two-Component System

NewRoof applies directly over your existing roof. The two-component system installs without penetrating the existing roof surface - using side fasteners for a watertight seal.

Step 1 - Insulation

Two-inch thick polystyrene sheets are placed across the existing roof surface, providing thermal insulation and noise dampening.

Step 2 - Aluminum Cover

Contoured aluminum panels are secured over the insulation using fasteners through the home's side - no roof penetrations required.
