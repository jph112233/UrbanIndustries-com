Building Systems

Structural Insulated Panels (SIPs)

A high-performance building system for residential and light commercial construction. Insulating foam core sandwiched between structural OSB facings. Factory customized for virtually any design.

  • Exceptional strength and energy efficiency
  • Reduces construction time and jobsite waste
  • Lower HVAC requirements - continuous insulation barrier
  • Green building certified - mold and VOC free
  • ICC ES Report ESR-4695

Weather Protection

Patio Covers

All-aluminum arched and insulated patio covers. The insulated model delivers R 13-1/2 thermal efficiency - protecting against summer heat and winter cold. Snap Lock panels are ICC ES certified (ESR-4649).

  • Arched panels for structural integrity
  • 3-inch thick R 13-1/2 insulated option
  • Telescoping posts with gutter system
  • Optional rib bracing for heavy snow loads
