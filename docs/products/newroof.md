## Overview

NewRoof is an insulated aluminum roofing system designed for mobile homes. The product works by applying a new roof structure over the existing roofing.

## Problems It Solves

- Leaks
- Roof rumble (noise)
- Condensation
- High heating and cooling expenses

---

## Technical Description

The system consists of two components:

1. **Insulation Layer** - Two-inch thick polystyrene sheets placed on the existing roof
2. **Covering** - Contoured aluminum panels that protect the insulation

Fasteners are secured through the home's side rather than penetrating the roof surface. This design provides flexibility and durability for a long lasting, low maintenance, energy efficient roof system.
