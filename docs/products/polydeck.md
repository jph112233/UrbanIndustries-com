## Overview

Polydeck is a laminated flooring panel that serves as an economical solution for footers extending below frost lines.

## Construction Details

- Rests on 4" x 4" and 2" x 6" foundation supports
- Meets footer requirements while maintaining minimal construction time
- Creates an ideal attachment surface for enclosures
- Strong, warm, and above-ground installation
