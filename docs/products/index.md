## Product Lines

### SIPs (Structural Insulated Panels)
A high-performance building system for residential and light commercial construction.

### Bulk Bags
Custom polypropylene bags designed to handle packaging requirements for dry, flowable materials in bulk applications.

### Enclosures
Customizable enclosures with numerous configuration options including material selection, window and door sizing, room dimensions, roof types, and skylights.

### Patio Covers
Both insulated and non-insulated cover solutions to provide weather protection and shade for outdoor spaces.

### Polydeck
A laminated flooring panel - a simple, economical, insulated solution to the footer problem, providing a foundation surface for enclosure construction.

### NewRoof System
Helps restore aging mobile homes by addressing leaks, roof noise, and moisture condensation issues.
