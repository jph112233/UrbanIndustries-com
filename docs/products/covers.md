## Urban Arched Patio Cover

An all-aluminum patio cover featuring arched panels that enhance structural integrity while maintaining an elegant aesthetic. The design incorporates "boxed" or folded panels to prevent water infiltration at the residence. For extended projections, optional rib bracing provides additional support against heavy winter snow loads.

**Materials & Components:**
- 1.5-inch square extruded, fluted, painted telescoping posts
- Extruded mounting rails and support channels
- Gutter system with roll-formed house-style 2" x 3" downspouts
- Color-matched components with complementary trim options

---

## Insulated Patio Cover

A 3-inch thick energy-efficient patio cover solution. Available with either extruded thermally broken aluminum h-bar joiners or self-joining snap-locking panels. The system delivers R 13-1/2 thermal efficiency shielding against the summer sun's heat and the winter cold, making it suitable for sunroom or screen room expansion.

**Certification:** Snap Lock roof panels are tested and certified by ICC Evaluation Service (Report ESR-4649).
