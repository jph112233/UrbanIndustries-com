## Overview

SIPs represent a high-performance building system for residential and light commercial construction. These panels feature an insulating foam core sandwiched between structural facings, typically oriented strand board (OSB). Factory manufacturing enables customization for virtually any building design.

The benefits include exceptional strength, energy efficiency, and cost-effectiveness while reducing construction time, labor costs, and jobsite waste.

---

## When, Where, and Why to Use SIPs

Builders select SIPs to ensure warm, healthy environments with safe, cost-effective materials. Urban Industries is a premier SIP panel manufacturer in Ohio.

---

## Cost Considerations

SIPs construction costs approximately the same as traditional wood frame construction when accounting for labor savings and shorter timelines. Additional savings come from reduced HVAC system requirements, as SIPs "reduce heat loss by establishing a continuous barrier of rigid foam insulation."
